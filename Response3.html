My Own Thoughts

This post has made me much fonder of cows (actually it is mostly due to the WhatsApp photos and videos). You know I love lambs (and sheep), and that is the reason I don't eat their meat (though I also don't like the taste). The same goes for rabbits. Perhaps by the end of summer the same will be true of cows. I'd like to be truly fully pescatarian, but occasionally I do really want meat, and as this is about once every 4 months, I'm hesitant to stop myself. The real meat consumption on my end is other people feeding me. But fortunately that is also uncommon-ish.

It's an interesting thought. I don't think they realised that most of the cows won't have names, just numbers. And unfortunately I don't think people would care much after getting desensitised.
